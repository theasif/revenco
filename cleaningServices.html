Cleaning services

Do you need a deep cleaning for your property or office? REVENCO offers top-quality cleaning services to keep your space neat and fresh. Whether it's your home, office, or factory, we make sure everything is spotless. Our team uses the best cleaning tools and safe, eco-friendly products to give you a clean and healthy environment.

We know how important it is to have a clean space to feel comfortable and productive. Whether you need regular cleaning or a deep clean, we can work around your schedule.

Professional deep cleaning

Do you need a deep cleaning for your property or office? We can send our well-trained team to carry out the service at your convenience. No matter how big or small your property is! REVENCO provides a full range of deep cleaning services.

Professional office cleaning

REVENCO is a leading company in office cleaning and our professional office cleaners are highly trained and experience assuring you the best possible results. We provide the highest quality and reliable office cleaning service. We guarantee that once they cleaned everything will be perfectly clean.

Commercial Glass Cleaning

At REVENCO, we provide commercial glass cleaning to help businesses keep their spaces neat and professional. Clean and clear windows make your building look better and create a friendly and inviting space for your customers, workers, and guests.

Commercial carpet cleaning

REVENCO offers top-quality carpet cleaning services to keep your workplace fresh and professional. Our team uses advanced cleaning methods to remove dirt, stains, and allergens, ensuring your carpets look and feel brand new.

Regular carpet cleaning not only enhances the appearance of your space but also improves indoor air quality for a healthier environment. We work with businesses of all sizes, providing fast and efficient service with minimal disruption.

Trust REVENCO to extend the life of your carpets and create a cleaner, more inviting space for your clients and employees.

After Building Cleaning

REVENCO also offers after building cleaning services to make your new or renovated space look spotless. Our team is skilled at cleaning up after construction, removing dust, debris, and leftover materials.

We focus on every detail, from windows to floors, ensuring your building is ready for use. Whether it’s a small office or a large commercial space, we handle it all with care and efficiency.

Choose REVENCO for thorough cleaning that prepares your property for a fresh start.

Tenancy Cleaning

We offer tenancy cleaning services to help make your move-in or move-out process smooth and stress-free. Our team provides thorough cleaning of all areas, including kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces, ensuring the property is spotless.

Tenancy cleaning in your house needs to be touched that includes:

  • Cleaning the microwave

  • Mop wooden or vinyl floors and etc.

Choose REVENCO for affordable, reliable cleaning services in Bangladesh. Let us take care of the cleaning so you can focus on what matters most.
