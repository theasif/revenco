Criminal Checks (AFI Switch)

At REVENCO, we offer criminal enquiry service that is linked to the SAPS automated fingerprint identification system. This mobile service can confirm whether or not a person has criminal record or not within 48 hours compared to weeks or months with other services. The AFI switch transmits captured fingerprint templates directly to the SAPS AFIS for identification purposes. In turn, AFI Switch delivers an illicit actively status report to the client. AFI switch provides unrivalled accuracy and speed over conventional paper-based services. We provide quick and reliable results to give you peace of mind.

Polygraph Testing

Polygraph testing is the quickest, most cost effective, accurate and reliable way to verify whether a person is being truthful or not. Polygraph testing is used for pre-employment screening, as a determination of management and staff integrity as well as a specific aid to investigations. Polygraph testing measures 3 bodily activities (breathing, heart rate & sweat activity) where other methods only use one (for example, vocal cords) making it easier to determine deception.

Layered voice analysis

Layered voice analysis (VLA) is technology that record psycho phycological stress responses in a human voice. A person suffers psychological stress when under pressure, i.e when being asked a question that instills fear of exposure. The consequences of lying and being exposed triggers a ‘high stress’ voice signature which the layered voice analyzer then records. Many intelligences agencies as well as private forensic psycho physiologists worldwide utilize layered voice analysis in preference to polygraph to technology.
