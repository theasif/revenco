This is a private firm established in 2016 named“REVENCO”. REVENCO is licensed by the Dhaka City Corporation. The Trade Licensed of the Dhaka South City Corporation is renewed year to year (Financial year basis: July- Jun). The Firm has up to date trade license for the current financial year 2019-2020.

Our Mission

REVENCO has a clear, strategic vision that the enables us to deliver the highest quality service to our clients whilst creating a happy working environment for our staff.

Our Vision

Our vision is to contribute to a safer, cleaner, and more efficient environment for businesses of all sizes, from small enterprises to large industrial complexes.

Our Stratgy

Our strategy focuses on understanding the unique needs of each client and tailoring our services to meet those needs effectively. We aim to build long-lasting relationships by consistently delivering high-quality results and adapting to changes in the industry.

We are full of wisdom and experience both in security and the cleaning industry. We aim to provide a quality service each and every time at the best price guaranteed! This is how we continue to grow our client base on a monthly basis.

REVENCO is a leading provider of out sourced facilities services with a long-standing reputation as a dependable company. Whether your facility is a commercial office complex, and institutional or industrial facility, or simply a small business, we offer a full-range of contract and on-demand facility of security service & cleaning service.

We proudly serve clients in various sectors, including aviation, banking, real estate, construction, government, retail, sports venues, event management, shopping malls, and manufacturing. Whether you're managing a small office or a large industrial complex, REVENCO is here to meet all your facilities management needs.

Our Commitment to Quality

At REVENCO, we understand that quality is key to building long-term relationships with our clients. We continuously invest in training and development to ensure that our team is equipped with the latest skills and knowledge. Our commitment to quality is reflected in every service we provide, from daily cleaning routines to advanced security measures.

Join the REVENCO Family

REVENCO’s client base is growing steadily each month, thanks to our dedication to providing outstanding services at competitive prices. We pride ourselves on our ability to adapt to the needs of each client, and we are always looking for new opportunities to serve businesses across various industries. If you’re looking for a reliable partner for your security and cleaning needs, REVENCO is here to help.
